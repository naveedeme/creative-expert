How to Accept Payments in Pakistan: Complete Guide

May 15, 2023 Naveed Ahmed 8 Comments

Accepting payments is one of the biggest challenges Pakistani e-commerce entrepreneurs face. In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore all available options with their pros and cons.

1. Mobile Payment Solutions

JazzCash: The most popular option with 45% market share. Setup requires a business account with documentation. Transaction fees: 2.9% + Rs. 10.

EasyPaisa: Telenor's solution with slightly lower fees (2.5% + Rs. 10) but smaller merchant network.

2. Bank Payment Gateways

HBL Pay: HBL's solution with 3% transaction fee. Requires maintaining an HBL business account.

UBL Omni: Good for COD reconciliation with 2.75% fees for card payments.

3. International Solutions

Payoneer: Can receive international payments but has high withdrawal fees (up to $30 per transfer).

Stripe via Third Parties: Services like PayPro Global allow access to Stripe with 5-7% additional fees.

4. Cash on Delivery (COD)

Still accounts for 65% of Pakistani e-commerce. Key points:

  • Average return rate: 25-35%
  • Use couriers with COD services (TCS, Leopards, Trax)
  • Factor in 3-5 days for payment settlement

For most Pakistani stores, we recommend a combination of JazzCash/EasyPaisa for digital payments and COD with a reliable courier partner.
